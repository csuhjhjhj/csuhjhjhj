## About Me

I am a PhD student in Cyberspace Security at Beihang University, with a research and engineering focus on intelligent agent systems, retrieval-augmented generation, LLM security, and recommendation algorithms.

My recent work connects research ideas with runnable systems: graph-enhanced RAG for manufacturing knowledge management, agent-native security analysis, backdoor detection for LoRA-finetuned LLMs, and LLM-enhanced recommendation models.

Previously, I worked on DeepSeek performance modeling at Huawei, advertising algorithm strategy at Meituan, and search infrastructure at Baidu.
